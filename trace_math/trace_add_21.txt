MAS FINAL ANSWER: \(\frac{1}{3}\)
TRUE FINAL ANSWER: $\frac{1}{3}$

---------
TRUE SOLUTION: Using $\sin ^{2} \theta+\cos ^{2} \theta=1$ gives $\cos ^{2} \theta=\frac{64}{T^{2}}$, so to maximize the sum, take $\cos \theta=\frac{8}{|T|}$. Using the formula for the sum of an infinite geometric series gives $\frac{8 /|T|}{1-8 /|T|}=\frac{8}{|T|-8}$. With $T=32$, the answer is $\frac{8}{24}=\frac{1}{3}$.
