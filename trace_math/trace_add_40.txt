MAS FINAL ANSWER: \( [LEO] = 10\sqrt{6} \)
TRUE FINAL ANSWER: 7

---------
TRUE SOLUTION: Let the side length of square base $E O J S$ be $2 x$, and let $M$ be the midpoint of $\overline{E O}$. Then $\overline{L M} \perp \overline{E O}$, and $L M=\sqrt{(5 \sqrt{2})^{2}-x^{2}}$ by the Pythagorean Theorem. Thus $[L E O]=\frac{1}{2} \cdot 2 x \sqrt{(5 \sqrt{2})^{2}-x^{2}}=$



$x \sqrt{(5 \sqrt{2})^{2}-x^{2}}$. With $T=4, x=1$, and the answer is $1 \cdot \sqrt{50-1}=\mathbf{7}$.
